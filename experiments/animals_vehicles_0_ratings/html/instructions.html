INSTRUCTIONS

PLEASE BE PATIENT!

The task will take a few seconds to load (around 40 MB). Unless you have a slow connection, it should take less than two minutes in total. If it takes more than 10 minutes, abort the experiment and contact us via hiplab@psy.ox.ac.uk.

Description

Thank you for participating in our study. In this experiment you'll see images of various objects and provide ratings, for example how dangerous or big they are. Detailed instructions will be provided once you go into full screen and click on the "start" button.

Mouse/Touchpad requirement

(Note that you will need a mouse (or touchpad of your laptop) to participate in our task. It may work on a touchscreen device such as a tablet/iPad, but we don't officially support these devices. If you are on a phone, this task will unfortunately not work. This task also does not work on Safari Browser. Please use alternatives such as Firefox or Chrome. Thanks for your understanding!

Full-screen requirement

We ask you to keep the window on fullscreen mode for the whole length of the experiment. This is because it is a psychological experiment and we need you to complete the experiment without distraction or doing other things at the same time. If you leave the fullscreen mode the experiment will be cancelled.

Duration and payment

During this experiment, you will need to give a fixed number of responses. The task is self-paced. The total duration of the experiment is estimated to be 40-50 min. However, even though the duration of the whole experiment depends on how quickly you respond, we ask you to try your best, rather than just giving quick but careless responses!
Once you finish with the task, you will receive a code that you can copy and submit to Prolific in order for us to accept your HIT.

Get ready!

When you have read this, you can move forward with the instructions. Good luck!
